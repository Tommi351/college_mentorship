Tips for a Successful Mentorship Session

  • 1. Prepare specific questions or topics you want to discuss
  • 2. Review your mentor's background and expertise beforehand
  • 3. Be punctual and respectful of the mentor's time
  • 4. Take notes during the session for future reference
  • 5. Follow up with any additional questions after the session
